In today's digital age, technology and gadgets play a major role in the lives of children. Children are exposed to various technological devices from a young age, and these devices are increasingly being integrated into their daily lives. While technology and gadgets can provide numerous benefits and opportunities for learning, they also have the potential to negatively impact the studies and development of children.

One of the ways technology and gadgets can affect the studies of children is through the distraction they provide. With the constant access to smartphones, tablets, and computers, children can easily become distracted and lose focus on their studies. Social media, video games, and other entertainment options can provide a constant source of distraction, leading children to spend more time on these devices and less time on their schoolwork. This can result in decreased academic performance and a lack of focus and attention in the classroom.
