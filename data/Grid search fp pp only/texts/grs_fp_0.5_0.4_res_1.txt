Moreover, providing accommodations for disabled students is not just a moral imperative but also a legal requirement under various laws such as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) in the United States. Universities have a legal obligation to provide reasonable accommodations for students with disabilities in order to ensure equal access to education.
