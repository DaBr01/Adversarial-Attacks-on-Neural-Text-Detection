In today's digital age, technology and gadgets have become an integral part of our daily lives. From smartphones to laptops, children are surrounded by various forms of technology that can both aid and hinder their studies. Technology has the potential to revolutionize education by providing access to vast amounts of information and resources at the click of a button. However, it also poses challenges in terms of distractions and negative impacts on learning.

One way in which technology affects the studies of children is through improved access to information. The internet allows students to research topics quickly and efficiently, enabling them to find answers to their questions in a matter of seconds. This can help enhance their understanding of complex concepts and improve academic performance. Additionally, educational websites and apps provide interactive lessons that cater to different learning styles, making studying more engaging and effective.

On the other hand, technology can also be a source of distraction for children during their study time. With social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, etc., always just a tap away on their smartphones or tablets, it can be challenging for kids to stay focused on schoolwork. The constant notifications from messaging apps or games can disrupt their concentration and impact their ability to retain information effectively.

Another aspect worth considering is how excessive screen time affects children's overall well-being.
